Code_Rush

Aurganon'24

17/04/24 10:00 AM BMS 7th Floor Programming Lab 3 150

Event Description

Code, Compete & Create! Problem Solving Showdown! Participants will be presented with a series of intriguing coding challenges designed to stretch their mental faculties and coding acumen. It is a team- based coding contest, where you'll have the opportunity to showcase your coding skills and creativity in a series of exciting challenges.
This event will happen in two rounds – Prelims and Finals.

Event Rules

• Wear your College ID Card
• Participants must adhere to the submission guidelines provided by the organizing committee.
• This is a team-based coding contest open to all batches and departments.
• Eligible students can participate in a team of 3 members
•The code is expected to be executed on time; late executions will not be evaluated!
• Plagiarism or copying code from external sources without proper attribution is strictly prohibited and may result in disqualification.
• Participants must be punctual and present at the designated match locations at the scheduled times.
