09/04/24 10:00 AM Gallery Hall 3 2 100

Event Description

ROUND 1: Pen and Paper Round
Teams tackle printed questions within a stipulated time. The lowest-scoring team bids farewell.

ROUND 2: Two-in-One Round
Segment 1: Audio-Visual Round: Teams decipher questions based on audio and visual clues.

Segment 2: Advanced Technical Quiz: Dive into the latest tech advancements and concepts.
Correct answers propel teams to the next level of competition.

ROUND 3: Rapid Fire Round

Event Rules

• Wear your College ID Card
• Participants will be given a specified amount of time to answer each question or complete each round of the quiz.
• Participants must be punctual and present at the designated match locations at the scheduled times.
• Hackers are strictly prohibited, and any form of foul play leads to disqualification.
