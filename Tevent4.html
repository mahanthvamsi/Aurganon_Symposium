Code Craft

Aurganon'24

17/04/24 10:00 AM Gallery Hall 2 2 100

Event Description

CodeCraft is an exciting web design challenge where you can showcase your creativity and coding skills by crafting a front-end web page using HTML and CSS. No prior experience needed. Participants will receive a theme for their web page design, from nature to technology.
During the design phase, you'll have time to bring your vision to life with HTML and CSS. Consider layout, colors, fonts, and overall design aesthetics to make your creation stand out. Let your creativity and imagination flow!

Event Rules

• Wear your College ID Card
• Participants will be given a specified amount of time to design and develop their websites.
• Participants must be punctual and present at the designated match locations at the scheduled times.
• Hackers are strictly prohibited, and any form of foul play leads to disqualification.
