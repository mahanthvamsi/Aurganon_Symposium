Craftopia

Aurganon'24

17/04/24 10:00 AM BMS 7th Floor Multimedia Lab 2 100

Event Description

LDesign, Devise & Display Craftopia presents a thrilling poster making contest where participants are invited to unleash their creativity using the Figma tool. Topics will be revealed on the spot, encouraging contestants to think on their feet and bring their innovative ideas to life through captivating designs.

Event Rules

• Wear your College ID Card
• Participants should bring their own laptop.
• Each team should consists of 2 members in a group.
• The design statement will be given on the spot.
• Participants must design an app or website based on that statement.
