Event Description

Capture the Flag (CTF) is a competitive hacking game where participants compete to find and exploit vulnerabilities in websites or web-applications to capture a "flag", which is a piece of hidden data or a specific file. Flags in CTFs are often represented as unique strings of characters, such as "FLAG{aurganon'24}", and they are hidden within the challenges.

Event Rules

• Wear your College ID Card
• Teams must consist of [number] members, with each member having a unique identifier.
• Participants must adhere to ethical hacking standards any malicious activity will result in immediate disqualification.
• Respect the privacy and security of other participants and the event infrastructure.
• The decisions of the judges are final.
