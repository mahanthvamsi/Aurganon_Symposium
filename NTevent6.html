Fandom Quizz

Aurganon'24

17/04/24 10:00 AM Gallery Hall 4 2 100

Event Description

In the Silver Screen Trivia (Fandom Quiz) event, participating teams will be asked a set of questions about Movies, Anime, Web series, TV shows, Cartoons, Novels, etc from various Indian silver screen industries. The Quiz comprises 2 rounds in a buzzer format.

Event Rules

• There will be 2 Rounds namely Prelims and Finals.
• The questions will be asked only in English.
• The questions may be from English or any Indian cine industry.
• The team which hits the buzzer first, will get the chance to answer first. If the answer is wrong, the question will be passed to the next team.
• No negative points . Each correct answer will carry one point. Bonus questions will carry two points.
• The same format will be followed for the tie breaker round.
• Winners and Runners are selected based on the points they acquire in each round.
