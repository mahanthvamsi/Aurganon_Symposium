Event Description

Innovate, collaborate Sprint! It is an interesting and fun filled team-based hackathon to showcase your innovation and creativity with your fellow coders.

• In the First half hour all the teams should complete and submit the Presentation.
• Topic: Own choice, participants can choose any topic.
• After the presentation, 1.5hrs will be given to code choose topic accordingly(Minimum 5 slides).
• 10:30 AM to 11:00 AM ( Participants can make Presentation ).
• 11:00 AM to 1:30 PM ( Coding time will be given ).
• Slots will be provided on First Come First Serve.
• Limited Slots are available.
