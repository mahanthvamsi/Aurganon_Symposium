Event Description

Push your limits, conquer obstacles, and emerge stronger than ever before. Are you ready to sweat, strive, and succeed? Don't miss this exhilarating opportunity to showcase your fitness!
This challenge consist of Push up, Plank and Squats. Every single participant should do all three challenges until their limits and each challenge has points based on reps and time. Winner and runner will be provided with Cash prize.
PUSH UPS - 1 REP = 2 POINTS
PLANK - 30sec = 5 POINTS
SQUATS - 1 REP = 2 POINTS

Event Rules

• Wear your College ID Card
• Events may have judges to ensure that each repetition meets the criteria for proper form and full range of motion.
• The decision of the judges is final and binding on all participants.
