Treasure Hunt

Aurganon'24

09/04/24 10:00 AM BMS 803 3 150

Event Description

Embark on an epic adventure in our treasure hunt game! The game consists of 8 clues. The first 5 teams to find the clues will be moved to the next round while the rest will be eliminated from the game. The rules for a treasure hunt are designed to make it a fair and fun experience for everyone. Teams are out in a public setting and must really police themselves to ensure that everyone complies with the rules.

Event Rules

• A team must contain maximum of 4 and minimum of 3 members.
• No sharing of clues.
• ID card is mandatory.
• The Treasure Hunt consists of 8 clues, each one leading to the next clue.
• The clues are to be found in a particular order.
• A team cannot skip a clue.
• Skipping a clue leads to the disqualification of the team.
• There is a time limit of 2 hrs for all the competing teams to find the treasure.
